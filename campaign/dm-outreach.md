## Core Rules (applies to every DM)

- **Short.** Nobody reads long DMs.
- **Specific.** Reference something they actually said, posted, or downloaded.
- **Low-friction ask.** "Want an invite?" not "Jump on a 30-min call?"
- **No pitch in opener.** Build rapport, then invite.
- **Sign with first name only.** No titles. No link in bio. Nothing.
- **No emojis in opener.** OK after a reply.
- **No em-dashes. No "unlock", "delve", "supercharge".**

## A/B Test Protocol

For each segment: send opener A to 50% of the list, opener B to 50%.

1. Reply rate (any response)
2. Positive reply rate (interested OR registered)
3. Registration rate (attributed via `?ref=dm-{segment}-{variant}`)

After 30 messages per variant, pivot 100% of remaining outreach to the winner.

Report daily in team Slack by 6pm Finland.

## Anti-Patterns (NEVER do these)

- ❌ "Hope you're doing well"
- ❌ "I came across your profile"
- ❌ Pitching in the opener ("I help businesses with X")
- ❌ Links in first message (Segments C/D especially — kills reply rate)
- ❌ Voice notes (save for post-reply conversations)
- ❌ "Synergy", "value-add", "ROI", "leverage", "circle back"
- ❌ 30-minute call asks in opener
- ❌ Copy-paste that reads templated (personalize 1 line minimum)
