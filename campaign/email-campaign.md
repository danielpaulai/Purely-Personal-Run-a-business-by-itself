## Voice Rules (every email)

- No em-dashes
- No "unlock", "delve", "supercharge", "game-changer", "seamless", "elevate", "leverage"
- Short sentences. One idea per line.
- Every email ends with a P.S.
- First name only in greetings
- Sign off "Danny" (not "Daniel", not "The Team")
- Specific numbers always
- No emoji in subject lines
- Every CTA is UTM-tagged (`?ref=email-{sequence}-{n}`)
