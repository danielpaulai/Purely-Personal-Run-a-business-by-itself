## The 2-day arc

```
DAY 1 · TEACH (foundation · 2 engines · the week)
       ↓
       Plant AI CEO Platform seed 3 times · NO pitch
       ↓
DAY 2 · TEACH (3 more engines · 25 min total · rapid)
       ↓
       AI CEO Platform REVEAL (live demo · NO price · 20 min)
       ↓
       THE PITCH (50 min · the entire afternoon)
       ↓
       Close + Q&A + graduation
```

## Why this structure

- Day 1 builds proof. Students RUN engines on their own business and see real output.
- Day 1 plants the AI CEO seed 3 times so the pitch on Day 2 is not a surprise.
- Day 2 morning recovers from Day 1 (wins share) + delivers 3 more engines fast.
- The AI CEO REVEAL at minute 40 of Day 2 is content · not a pitch · just demos.
- After the break, students self-select to stay for the offer.
- The 50-min pitch follows the 7 P's compressed and uses LIVE DEMOS as the proof.

## The core principle

**42% of Day 2 is the pitch.** That is correct. The pitch is what funds 12 months of business. Don't undersize it. Don't apologize for it.
