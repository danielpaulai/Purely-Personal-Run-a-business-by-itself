# The Wealth Mindset · 17 Patterns That Separate Wealthy and Broke

> The single biggest predictor of financial outcome isn't strategy
> or tactics · it's the mental patterns running underneath. 17
> patterns. Each one is a fork in the road. Wealthy operators take
> one path. Broke operators take the other. Same opportunities.
> Different decisions.

---

## How to use this file

Read each pattern. For each, ask: "Which side am I on TODAY?" Be
honest. The pattern that's furthest from where you want to be is the
one to work on this quarter.

You can't fix all 17 at once. Pick 1-2. Practice them for 90 days.
Move to the next.

---

## The 17 patterns

### 1 · Create vs Wait

```
WEALTHY:  "I create my opportunities."
BROKE:    "Opportunities will come to me."
```

Wealthy operators don't wait for clients to find them. They build
the pipeline. They send the cold message. They run the campaign.

**Practice:** today, create ONE opportunity that wouldn't exist if
you hadn't acted (a DM · an offer · a partnership pitch).

---

### 2 · Play to Win vs Play Not to Lose

```
WEALTHY:  "I'm focused on what I want to BUILD."
BROKE:    "I'm focused on not losing what I have."
```

Risk-aversion at the wrong stage of life capped most operators at
their current income. Defensive money is fine after $5M net worth.
Before that · play offense.

**Practice:** ask "what would I do if I were 10x more confident
right now?" Then do that thing.

---

### 3 · Committed to Wealth vs Hoping for Wealth

```
WEALTHY:  "I'm committed to being wealthy. Whatever it takes."
BROKE:    "I want to be wealthy · if it's not too hard."
```

Commitment vs hope. Commitment means the actions happen
unconditionally. Hope means the actions happen IF circumstances are
right.

**Practice:** write down 3 specific actions you've been "hoping" to
take. Commit to one. Calendar block it.

---

### 4 · Think Big vs Think Small

```
WEALTHY:  "I think in terms of millions."
BROKE:    "I think in terms of hundreds."
```

The gap isn't ambition · it's RANGE. Wealthy operators use bigger
denominators. They think in 12-month outcomes, not week outcomes.

**Practice:** translate every weekly goal into its 12-month
equivalent. ($100/wk → $5K/year. Where do I want $5K to go?)

---

### 5 · Focus on Opportunities vs Focus on Obstacles

```
WEALTHY:  "What's the OPPORTUNITY here?"
BROKE:    "What could go wrong?"
```

Both questions are valid. The DEFAULT question matters. Wealthy
operators default to "where's the upside?" Broke operators default
to "where's the risk?"

**Practice:** when a new opportunity arrives, list the 3 best-case
outcomes BEFORE listing the risks.

---

### 6 · Admire Other Successful People vs Resent Them

```
WEALTHY:  "I admire what they built. I want to learn from them."
BROKE:    "They got lucky / had connections / cheated."
```

Resentment of successful people is a tax on your future success.
You can't become what you secretly hate. Admire openly. Study them.
Steal their patterns.

**Practice:** unfollow accounts that make you feel small. Follow
accounts that make you feel inspired by the work.

---

### 7 · Associate with Successful People vs Associate with Negative People

```
WEALTHY:  "I'm intentional about my circle."
BROKE:    "My friends are who life put around me."
```

The 5 people you spend the most time with shape your average. This
isn't elitism · it's environmental design.

**Practice:** join one community / cohort / mastermind this quarter
where the room makes you feel slightly behind.

---

### 8 · Promote Yourself vs Hide

```
WEALTHY:  "I share what I'm building."
BROKE:    "I don't want to seem braggy."
```

Self-promotion isn't ego · it's information. People can't refer you
if they don't know what you do. Visibility is a form of generosity ·
you're saving them from not knowing you exist.

**Practice:** post one specific thing you shipped this week. Don't
hedge. Don't apologize. Just ship + tell.

---

### 9 · Bigger than Problems vs Smaller than Problems

```
WEALTHY:  "I'm bigger than this problem · I can solve it."
BROKE:    "This problem is too big. I can't."
```

Same problem. Different self-concept. Wealthy operators see
themselves as larger than circumstances. Broke operators feel
crushed by them.

**Practice:** when you feel overwhelmed, say "this problem is X
big · I am 10X big." Then take the first action.

---

### 10 · Receive Well vs Receive Poorly

```
WEALTHY:  "Thank you. I appreciate it."
BROKE:    "Oh, I shouldn't have. Let me pay you back."
```

Bad receivers are blocked from gifts · including referrals,
introductions, money. The energy of receiving (with gratitude · not
shame) is what allows more to come.

**Practice:** next time someone gives you a compliment, just say
"thank you." Don't deflect. Don't return-volley. Just receive.

---

### 11 · Choose Get-Paid-on-Results vs Get-Paid-on-Time

```
WEALTHY:  "Pay me on outcomes I deliver."
BROKE:    "Pay me by the hour."
```

Hourly pricing caps income at hours × rate. Outcome pricing
unlocks unlimited upside. The mental shift: you're worth what you
DELIVER · not how LONG it takes.

**Practice:** for one engagement this quarter, structure pricing as
performance-based. See what changes.

---

### 12 · Both vs Either-Or

```
WEALTHY:  "How can I have BOTH?"
BROKE:    "I have to choose · A or B."
```

The "either/or" frame caps options. The "both/and" frame finds the
third path most people miss.

**Practice:** the next time you're choosing between two options ·
pause. Ask "is there a way to have both?" Spend 5 minutes finding
the third path before deciding.

---

### 13 · Net Worth vs Working Income

```
WEALTHY:  "I'm building net worth."
BROKE:    "I'm earning a paycheck."
```

Income is what you trade time for. Net worth is what compounds.
Most knowledge workers focus on income their whole career · then
wonder why they're not wealthy at 60. Net worth is the actual
metric.

**Practice:** track net worth monthly · not just income. Make at
least one investment per quarter (even small).

---

### 14 · Manage Money Well vs Mismanage

```
WEALTHY:  "I track every dollar · I deploy strategically."
BROKE:    "I don't know where my money goes."
```

Profit First, 90-day forecasts, monthly reviews · these aren't
"things to do later when I'm rich." They're the discipline that
GETS you rich.

**Practice:** if you don't know your bank balance right now · check
in 60 seconds. Then commit to checking daily for 30 days.

---

### 15 · Money Works for You vs You Work for Money

```
WEALTHY:  "I deploy money to earn money."
BROKE:    "I trade time for money."
```

This is the I-quadrant transition. Until your money is making
money, you're capped at active income. The first $10K of investment
income compounds forever.

**Practice:** allocate 5-10% of monthly income to investments
(stocks, real estate, equity in someone else's business · anything
that earns without your time).

---

### 16 · Act in Spite of Fear vs Frozen by Fear

```
WEALTHY:  "I'm scared. I do it anyway."
BROKE:    "I'm scared. I'll wait."
```

Wealthy operators are also scared. They've just decoupled fear from
inaction. Fear is data · not a stop sign.

**Practice:** identify ONE thing you've been putting off because
of fear. Set a deadline (this week). Do it.

---

### 17 · Constantly Learn and Grow vs Think You Know

```
WEALTHY:  "I'm a permanent student."
BROKE:    "I already know enough."
```

The moment you think you've figured it out · you stop growing. The
wealthiest people read more, listen more, ask more questions, take
more courses than the broke do.

**Practice:** invest in one piece of education per quarter · book,
course, cohort, conference. Implement what you learn.

---

## How to install a wealth pattern

You don't change a pattern by reading about it. You install it by
PRACTICING it.

The 30-day pattern install:

```
WEEK 1 · Notice when the old pattern fires.
WEEK 2 · Catch the old pattern · pause · choose the new pattern.
WEEK 3 · The new pattern starts to feel natural.
WEEK 4 · The new pattern becomes the default.
```

Don't try to install all 17 at once. Pick ONE. Practice for 30
days. Move to the next.

## The 1-question diagnostic

When facing any decision · pause and ask:

> "What would the wealthier version of me do here?"

That's the answer. The decisions you make today (50+ per day)
compound into either the wealthy path or the broke path. The
patterns aren't theory · they're the daily fork in the road.

The engines do execution. YOU do the patterns. Together · the
business that runs without you · funded by the wealth mindset that
chose to build it.
