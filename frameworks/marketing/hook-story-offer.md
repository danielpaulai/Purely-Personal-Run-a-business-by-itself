# Hook · Story · Offer (HSO)

> The 3-part structure underneath every piece of content that sells.
> Works on LinkedIn, email, ads, video, sales pages, voice memos.
> Three parts. Always in order. Never skipped.

---

## The structure

```
1 · HOOK      · stops the scroll · earns 5 more seconds
2 · STORY     · earns belief · transports them into the outcome
3 · OFFER     · gives them ONE next move
```

If the hook fails, the story doesn't get read. If the story fails, the
offer doesn't get clicked. The order is non-negotiable.

---

## 1 · HOOK · stops the scroll (the first 1-2 lines)

Goal: earn 5 more seconds of attention. That's it. Not click. Not
buy. Just **don't scroll past.**

A good hook has 3 things stacked:
- **Specificity** · numbers, names, dates
- **Pattern interrupt** · breaks the expectation of the feed
- **Implied stake** · "what's at risk if I scroll past?"

7 working hook patterns:

### a · Personal proof
"I 10x'd my pipeline in 7 days. Here's exactly how."

### b · Quiet truth
"Most founders don't have a marketing problem. They have a writing problem."

### c · Stat contrast
"$2M in revenue. $0 in marketing spend. One thing changed."

### d · Contrarian
"Stop building 'high-ticket coaching offers.' Build cash-flowing assets instead."

### e · How-to
"How to write a LinkedIn post that gets 100k views in 7 minutes."

### f · Mistake/regret
"I wasted 18 months building the wrong product. Here's what I'd do differently."

### g · Question
"What if your business could run for 7 days without you · and grow?"

**Rule:** The first 210 characters MUST carry the full hook. LinkedIn
truncates at "see more" at ~210 chars. If the hook breaks mid-word,
you've already lost.

---

## 2 · STORY · earns belief (the body)

Goal: make them believe the dream is for THEM. Not generic. Not
abstract. Specific person, specific moment, specific transformation.

Two structures that work:

### Pattern A · Before-Bridge-After (BBA)

```
BEFORE    · "Last quarter I was sending 50 cold DMs a day. Closing 1."
BRIDGE    · "Then I rebuilt my qualification with one filter."
AFTER     · "Now I send 5 DMs · close 3."
```

### Pattern B · Problem-Agitate-Solution (PAS)

```
PROBLEM   · "Most coaches charge $5k. Make $40k. Burn out by month 9."
AGITATE   · "Because every dollar requires a sales call. Every call drains you."
SOLUTION  · "I rebuilt my offer as a self-serve product. Same revenue. Zero calls."
```

**Rule:** The story must be specific enough to feel true. Generic
stories ("many founders struggle with...") read as AI slop. Use
proper nouns, real numbers, named people, exact dollar amounts.

---

## 3 · OFFER · one next move (the CTA)

Goal: ONE next move. Not three. Not "follow me + book a call + visit
my site." Pick the lowest-effort highest-relevance ask.

Hierarchy of CTAs (lowest commitment first):

| CTA type | Friction | When to use |
|---|---|---|
| **Comment trigger** | "Comment 'pipeline' for the playbook" | Top of funnel · LinkedIn |
| **DM bait** | "DM me 'audit' if this is you" | Mid funnel · interest signal |
| **Newsletter opt-in** | "Link in bio for the full breakdown" | Build the list |
| **Discovery call** | "Reply 'call' for a 15-min walk-through" | Warm prospect |
| **Direct buy** | "Tier 1 link in chat. Closes Sunday 13:00." | Active sales window |

**Rule:** The CTA energy must match the post energy. A high-vibe story
+ a hard buy CTA reads as bait. A vulnerable story + a $2K offer
reads as a setup. Match.

---

## The full template

```
HOOK    · 1-2 lines · specific number · stake clear

[blank line]

STORY   · 4-7 short paragraphs · BBA or PAS · proper nouns
        · Use real numbers · use named people
        · End with the bridge moment · the shift

[blank line]

OFFER   · ONE line · one next move
        · Action verb + outcome
        · Time-bound if possible

[blank line]

P.S.    · One question · or one line of context · or one provocation
```

---

## Apply it to /marketing-engine

When drafting any LinkedIn post, the engine MUST:

1. Generate 3 hooks (different patterns from a-g above)
2. For each hook, write a BBA or PAS story
3. End each post with one (and only ONE) CTA
4. Display all 3 variants
5. Let the user pick

Never deliver a draft without hook + story + offer. If any one is
weak, the post will not perform.

## Worked example · Purely Personal post

```
HOOK   · I built a business that runs without me in 4 hours.

STORY  · Last year I was running my coaching business 80 hours a
         week. Every Sunday night I'd lie awake planning Monday.
         Then I built 6 weekly routines · all powered by Claude.
         Marketing on Monday. Leads on Tuesday. Newsletter Wed.
         Friday wrap. Sunday plan. By Saturday morning I knew my
         entire next week was already done. Without me touching it.

OFFER  · I'm running a 2-day workshop May 2-3. $49.
         Comment 'autopilot' if you want the link.

P.S.   · This isn't a course. It's an install.
```

Hook = personal proof + specificity ("4 hours")
Story = BBA · before/bridge/after with real moments
Offer = comment trigger · one move · time-bounded
P.S. = reframe (positioning)
