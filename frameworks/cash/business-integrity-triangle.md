# The Business Integrity Triangle · 8 Pieces of a Real Business

> Most "businesses" are projects with revenue. A real business has 8
> integrities working together. Miss any one · the business is
> brittle. Get all 8 right · the business runs without you.

---

## The triangle structure

```
                    ┌─────────────────┐
                    │     MISSION     │
                    └────────┬────────┘
                             │
                  ┌──────────┴──────────┐
                  │                     │
                  ▼                     ▼
              ┌───────┐             ┌───────┐
              │ TEAM  │             │LEADER │
              └───┬───┘             └───┬───┘
                  │                     │
                  └──────────┬──────────┘
                             ▼
              ┌──────────────────────────────┐
              │  PRODUCT │ LEGAL │ SYSTEMS  │
              │  ── + ── │  ──   │  ──      │
              │   COMMS  │       │  CASH    │
              └──────────────────────────────┘
```

3 outside frame: **Mission, Team, Leader** (the vision + the people)
5 inside: **Product, Legal, Systems, Communications, Cash** (the
operations)

A real business has all 8. A side-project has 1-3. The transition
from side-project to business is the journey of installing the
remaining integrities.

## The OUTSIDE frame · Mission · Team · Leader

These are the WHY of the business. Without them, the inside
operations are a hollow shell.

### MISSION

The reason the business exists beyond making money. Two questions:

```
1 · WHO are we serving?
2 · WHAT transformation do we provide?
```

**Examples:**
- "We serve solopreneurs · we transform 80-hour weeks into 4-hour weeks via AI."
- "We serve B2B founders · we transform random sales calls into systematic pipeline."

A clear mission attracts:
- The right team
- The right customers
- The right capital
- The right partners

A fuzzy mission repels all four.

### TEAM

The people who do the work. For a solopreneur in 2026, "team" can
include:
- AI engines (Claude, GPT, Gemini · doing real work)
- Contractors (designer, developer, accountant)
- Virtual assistants
- Eventually: full-time hires
- Always: the leader (you)

**The team rule:** every role on the team should be CLEAR · with
specific outputs and accountability. "We'll figure it out" is the
death of team integrity.

For Purely Personal in 2026, the team might be:
- You (architect / operator / face)
- /marketing-engine (the marketing executive)
- /sales-engine (the sales executive)
- /operations-engine (the COO)
- /cash-engine (the CFO)
- /leadership-engine (the chief of staff)
- + 1 VA for human-only tasks
- + 1 designer (contract)
- + 1 accountant (contract)

That's a 9-person team for a solopreneur. AI does most of the work.
You manage the architects. The business looks like a 30-person
operation from the outside.

### LEADER

The one ultimately accountable. For solopreneurs · this is you.

**The leader's three jobs:**
1 · Strategy (where we're going)
2 · Capital allocation (where the money goes)
3 · Hiring + firing (who's on the team)

Everything else is delegate-able or automate-able. These three are
yours forever.

**The leader trap:** doing the work instead of leading. If you're
drafting LinkedIn posts manually 18 months in · you've abandoned the
leader role. You're back in S quadrant.

## The INSIDE operations · 5 integrities

These are the HOW of the business. The plumbing.

### PRODUCT

What you sell. The deliverable. The transformation in a box.

**4 sub-integrities of product:**
- **Specification** · what exactly does it deliver?
- **Quality** · is it consistently great?
- **Delivery** · how does it get to the customer?
- **Support** · what happens when something breaks?

For Purely Personal:
- Workshop is product 1 ($49)
- AI CEO program is product 2 ($1,999 / $2,999)
- Each must have all 4 sub-integrities solid

### LEGAL

The structure that protects the business and the founder.

**Must-have legal infrastructure:**
- Business entity (LLC, corp, etc.)
- Customer agreement (terms of service, refund policy)
- Privacy policy (GDPR + your jurisdiction)
- Contractor agreements (not handshake)
- IP protections (trademark · copyright on materials · NDA where
  needed)
- Tax filings (current, organized)
- Insurance (liability, errors & omissions for service businesses)

**The legal rule:** spend 5% of revenue on legal infrastructure
SETUP in year 1. Maintenance is cheap. The setup is what saves you.

### SYSTEMS

The processes that run without you. SOPs. Tools. Engines.

**The 5 engines + 6 routines = the systems integrity for Purely
Personal.**

A real business has DOCUMENTED systems · not just tribal knowledge in
the founder's head. If you got hit by a bus tomorrow, could someone
else run the business in 6 weeks? If no · systems integrity is
weak.

### COMMUNICATIONS

How information flows · inside and outside the business.

**Inside flow:**
- Team meetings (L10 · weekly)
- Async updates (Slack, Notion)
- Documentation (BUSINESS-BRAIN.md, SOPs, frameworks)

**Outside flow:**
- Customer communications (email, support, onboarding)
- Marketing (LinkedIn, newsletter, content)
- Sales (outreach, demos, follow-ups)

**Comms integrity test:** can a new team member find any answer in
under 5 minutes? Can a new customer get help in under 1 hour? If no
· comms is broken.

### CASH

The lifeblood. Without cash · all other integrities collapse.

**Cash integrity = ALL of:**
- Profit First system installed (5 accounts)
- 90-day cash flow forecast updated weekly
- Stripe / revenue accurately tracked
- Tax allocation current
- Owner pay sustainable
- Profit distributions happening quarterly
- War chest growing (PROFIT account)

The /cash-engine maintains cash integrity in the Purely Personal
system.

## The integrity audit (run quarterly)

Score each integrity 0-10:

```
OUTSIDE FRAME:
[ ] /10 · MISSION         (clear · who · what transformation)
[ ] /10 · TEAM            (right roles · clear outputs · accountable)
[ ] /10 · LEADER          (you doing strategy + capital + hiring)

INSIDE OPERATIONS:
[ ] /10 · PRODUCT         (spec + quality + delivery + support)
[ ] /10 · LEGAL           (entity + agreements + IP + tax)
[ ] /10 · SYSTEMS         (SOPs + engines + documented)
[ ] /10 · COMMS           (inside flow + outside flow)
[ ] /10 · CASH            (Profit First + forecast + allocations)

TOTAL: ___ /80
```

**Scoring interpretation:**
- 70-80 · real business · runs without daily founder presence
- 50-69 · transitional · founder-dependent · brittle
- 30-49 · S-quadrant operation dressed as a business
- < 30 · side project with revenue

The integrity below 7 is the bottleneck. Fix that one. Re-audit in
90 days.

## How to install missing integrities

Most operators have 4-5 of the 8 strong. Here's the typical fix
order:

1 · MISSION (write it down · 1 day)
2 · LEADER (define your 3 jobs · stop doing other people's work)
3 · CASH (Profit First setup · 1 week)
4 · LEGAL (incorporate · contracts · 1-2 weeks)
5 · SYSTEMS (document SOPs · install engines · 60 days)
6 · COMMS (set comms cadence · centralize docs · 30 days)
7 · PRODUCT (audit + improve · ongoing · 90 days)
8 · TEAM (right-size as B-quadrant grows · 6-18 months)

Total runway from "S-quadrant operator" to "real business" with all
8 integrities: 12-18 months of focused work. The engines compress
this · they're systems + comms + product (partially) + cash all in
one stack.
