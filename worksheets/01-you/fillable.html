◆ Guidelines

Before you ship Worksheet 1 ~8 min

Check every field. Real specifics only. No vague.

What to fill

  • Your current one-liner (Column 3)
  • Your rewritten line (Column 4) — sharper than Column 3
  • Insight — what you noticed comparing yours to Examples
  • 5 phrases you would NEVER say (real banned list)
  • Your last LinkedIn post opening — first 1–2 sentences
  • Tone in exactly 3 words

How to fill it well

  • Use specifics. "12 hours a month" beats "many hours"
  • Name your ICP inside your rewritten line
  • Banned phrases are words you would ACTUALLY never say — not hypothetical
  • Example post must be real. Paste from your LinkedIn.
  • Three tone words. Not a sentence.
  • Insight is honest — what's weak, what's missing, what to fix
