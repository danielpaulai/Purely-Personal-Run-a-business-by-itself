◆ Guidelines

Before you ship Worksheet 4 ~15 min

Real competitors. Real verbatim quotes. No paraphrasing.

What to fill

  • 3 competitor names + URLs
  • Each competitor's angle in one line
  • Your gap to own vs each one
  • 4 audience pain themes
  • Exact verbatim quotes (typos stay in)
  • Source for each verbatim

How to fill it well

  • Name real competitors — ones your prospects already compare you to.
  • "Their angle" is what their homepage says (one line).
  • "Gap to own" is what YOU will say that they don't — not what they "miss".
  • Verbatims are raw. Don't paraphrase. Typos stay in. Emoji stay in.
  • Sources are specific: "r/SaaS · Apr 2026" not "Reddit".
  • 4 themes max. Each distinct. Don't pick 4 variations of "AI sounds generic".
