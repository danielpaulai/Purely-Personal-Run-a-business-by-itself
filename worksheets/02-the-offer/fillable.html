◆ Guidelines

Before you ship Worksheet 2 ~7 min

Your offer must pass the 5-second test.

What to fill

  • Current offer line (Column 3)
  • Sharpened offer (Column 4)
  • Insight from comparing to Examples
  • Positioning sentence — why you vs anyone else
  • Starting price (exact number)
  • 90-day goal with a number
  • Daily key metric

How to fill it well

  • Offer ≤ 15 words. Verb + outcome + ICP.
  • No jargon. Must read in 5 seconds.
  • Positioning is one line. Why you vs everyone else.
  • Price is a number, not a range ("$49" not "$40–60")
  • 90-day goal must contain a quantifiable number (revenue, sign-ups, clients)
  • Daily metric is something you can check every morning in under 30 seconds
