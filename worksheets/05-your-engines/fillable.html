◆ Guidelines

Before you ship Worksheet 5 ~8 min

Everything needed to fire all 5 engines.

What to fill

  • 3–5 brand colors (hex codes)
  • Display font + body font
  • Visual voice in 3 words
  • 5 AI queries you want to rank on
  • 90-day priority (one sentence)
  • 3 tools in your daily stack

How to fill it well

  • Hex codes are exact. Swatches update live as you type — check each box fills with color.
  • Fonts you ACTUALLY use. Not aspirational. Look at your real site.
  • Visual voice = 3 adjectives. "Editorial, warm, precise" not a sentence.
  • AI queries = how someone would actually search. Natural language, not keywords.
  • Priority must contain a number (revenue, attendees, conversions).
  • Stack = tools you use DAILY. Not tools you've installed or plan to use.
