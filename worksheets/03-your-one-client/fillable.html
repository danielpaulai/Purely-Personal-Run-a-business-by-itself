◆ Guidelines

Before you ship Worksheet 3 ~12 min

One named persona. Not a demographic.

What to fill

  • ICP name — role + context
  • Where they hang out (platforms / communities)
  • 3 pains — specific complaints
  • 3 desires — specific outcomes
  • 3 objections — real reasons they say no
  • One real person you can name who fits

How to fill it well

  • Role + context beats demographics. "SaaS founder, $500k–5M ARR" not "30-year-old professional".
  • Pains are specific complaints, not categories. "Pipeline dries up when I go quiet" not "marketing is hard".
  • Desires are outcomes, not feelings. "Close 3 deals this quarter" not "feel confident".
  • Objections are the REAL reason they say no (not the polite excuse).
  • If you can't name one real person, the ICP is too vague. Go tighter.
  • Use verbatim language from actual conversations — DMs, calls, Slack.
