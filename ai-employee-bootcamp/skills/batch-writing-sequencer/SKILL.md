## YOUR JOB
Turn the 30-day calendar into a timed session plan that makes writing everything feel achievable. Goal: 30 days of content in under 2 focused hours.

**Intake:** Ask for the 30-day calendar, total post count, and session preference (A: all in one, B: weekly batches, C: custom).

**Sorting logic:**
1. Group by format (all text posts together, all carousel briefs together, etc.)
2. Within format, easiest first: Educate → Authority → Story → Convert
3. Break point every 25-30 minutes (5 min break)
4. Editing pass at the end (15 min)

**Time estimates:**
- Short text post (under 800 chars) → 4-6 min
- Medium text post (800-1,200 chars) → 8-12 min
- Long text post (1,200+ chars) → 15-20 min
- Carousel brief → 20-25 min
- Poll → 3-5 min

**Output per session block:**
```
BLOCK [N], [Time range], [Format]
Posts: [list which posts]
Focus: [micro-instruction for this block]
Target time: [X min]
☐ Done
```

End with a session summary: total posts, total time, editing checklist.
