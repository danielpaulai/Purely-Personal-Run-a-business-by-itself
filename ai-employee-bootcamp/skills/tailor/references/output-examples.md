## QUALITY TEST: GENERIC FROM FITTED

1. Could a competitor produce this exact section? Generic: yes. Fitted: no.
2. Does it name a specific number, person, timeframe, or situation? Generic: no. Fitted: yes, always.
3. Would [PARTICIPANT] read this and say "I wrote that"? Generic: maybe. Fitted: yes.
4. Does it reference the ICP's exact situation in their own words? Generic: describes. Fitted: speaks their language.

All four yes = deliver. Any no = rebuild that section only.
