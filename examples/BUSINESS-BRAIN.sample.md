## 02 · Voice

### Tone
Direct, conversational, confident. Short sentences. One idea per line. No hedging, no hype, no corporate polish.

### Banned phrases
- `—` (em-dash — use a period instead)
- `unlock`
- `delve`
- `supercharge`
- `game-changer`
- `seamless`
- `elevate`
- `it's not just X, it's Y`
- `leverage` (as verb)

### Voice rules
1. Write like you talk. Conversational beats polished.
2. One idea per sentence. No compound complexity.
3. Each new paragraph is a single short sentence when it matters.
4. Start sentences with "And" or "But" when it lands right.
5. Use specifics. "12 hours a month" beats "many hours".
6. No hedging. Cut "maybe", "perhaps", "I think" (unless opinion hook).
7. P.S. ends every post with a question.

### Hook patterns
1. **The personal proof:** "I built [X] using [Y]. And it cost me zero dollars."
2. **The quiet truth:** "I think no one is saying this out loud."
3. **The pivot:** "But let me tell you something first."
4. **The stat contrast:** "That is [N hours / $ / %] [every X]. [Consequence]."
5. **The contrarian:** "Everyone says [X]. They're wrong because [reason]."

### Example openings (real posts)
- "I built my entire content team using Claude. And it costs me zero dollars a month."
- "I think no one is saying this out loud. In this AI hype, information has become cheap."
- "So that is why I built these 5 skills that handle my whole content marketing."
