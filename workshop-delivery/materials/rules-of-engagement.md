# Rules of Engagement · the 7 rules · read aloud at 11:05 Saturday

These rules are non-negotiable for both days. We follow them or we don't ship.
Please read them now before Day 1.

---

## 1 · Camera on · always

You're paying to be here. So am I (with my time). We see each other or we
don't connect. Cameras off = you're not here.

**Exception:** bathroom, food, child-related emergency. Drop a "BRB" in chat.

## 2 · One screen for me · second screen for your work

You'll be in Claude Code AND watching slides AND in chat. Trying to do that
on one screen = you'll get nothing done. Two-monitor setup, or laptop +
tablet, or laptop + phone. Make it work.

## 3 · Mic muted unless I unmute you

Hot seats are voice. Q&A is chat. Everything else is muted. This isn't a
party · it's a build session.

## 4 · Chat is open · for these 4 things only

- Wins ("SHIPPED · here's the URL")
- Questions ("how do I...")
- Partner share replies (when I say "find a partner")
- Encouragement (✓ for someone else's win)

Not allowed: side conversations, off-topic memes, multi-day arguments,
unrelated promotions.

## 5 · Phone face-down · in another room ideally

If your phone vibrates · you've split your attention. The 30 minutes after a
phone-distraction is when you miss the most important thing. Phone in airplane
mode, in another room, ideally locked away. **Do this for both days.**

## 6 · If you ship · type SHIPPED in chat

Every time you publish a post, send an outreach, complete a routine — drop
SHIPPED in chat with the URL or screenshot. We celebrate every one.

This isn't optional. The public commitment IS the work.

## 7 · Be kind to your future self · do the work

You will be tempted to skip the partner share. To not raise the price. To not
publish the post. To "save it for later."

Don't.

Your future self is the one who paid for this. Honour them by being uncomfortable
for two days. That's the whole deal.

---

## What happens if you break these

| Rule broken | What I do |
|---|---|
| 1 (camera off) | DM you privately · "Are you OK? Need to step away for 5?" |
| 5 (phone interrupting) | Notice + remind in next break |
| Multiple over the weekend | I follow up Tuesday · we talk · usually it's life happening |

I won't kick anyone out for being human. I will follow up after the workshop
to make sure you're OK and got value.

Group reads them aloud together. Yes, on Zoom. Yes, with cameras on. It feels
weird. It works.
