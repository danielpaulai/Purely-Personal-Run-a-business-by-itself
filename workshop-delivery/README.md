## During the workshop · your "fail-safes"

If energy dies mid-afternoon:
1. Pull the next break forward by 15 min
2. Music + standing for 60 sec
3. Read 5 wins from chat aloud · name people

If your demo breaks live:
1. Don't apologize — laugh
2. "This is why we run quick_install_check"
3. Run it on your own system live · they see how the diagnostic works

If a participant is rude / dominating:
1. DM privately: "Hey, love the energy. Can you hold your next 2 questions
   for the Q&A? I want others to chime in."
2. Almost always works

If Zoom freezes:
1. Switch to Google Meet (have backup link in chat)
2. Lose 5 min · switch · continue
