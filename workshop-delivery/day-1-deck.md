## Block 1 · Act 1 · The Problem (11:30–12:30) · 6 slides

### [1.1] · The story (your 90-second origin)

```
TITLE:  How I got here.
SUB:    [your photo · before/after if you have it]
```

*Tell your origin story in **exactly 90 seconds**. Practice it. Time it. The
emotional shape matters more than the words: stuck → break → discovery →

### [1.2] · The solopreneur trap

```
TITLE: The trap.
BIG:   You are a 1-person company.
       That company has a Marketing department,
       a Sales department, an Ops department,
       a Finance department, and a CEO.
       
       Every department is YOU.
       And every department wants 8 hours of YOUR day.
```

*Pause. Let it land. They're nodding.*

### [1.3] · The math

```
TITLE: Do the math.
BODY:  5 departments × 8 hours = 40 hours per day.
       You have 12 productive hours.
       
       Something has to give.
       Usually it's the marketing.
       Or the sales follow-up.
       Or the cash forecast.
       Or your weekend.
       
       Or all four.
```

### [1.4] · The 5 engines

```
TITLE: Every business has 5 engines.

Engine 1 · MARKETING  (attract attention)
Engine 2 · SALES      (turn attention into revenue)
Engine 3 · OPERATIONS (deliver what you sold)
Engine 4 · CASH       (forecast + collect + reinvest)
Engine 5 · LEADERSHIP (decide what to do next)
```

*Show the diagram. Each engine = one card. Click in one at a time. Build it visually.*

### [1.5] · Why "AI tools" don't fix this

```
TITLE: Most AI tools = a screwdriver in a hardware store.
BODY:  ChatGPT is great. But it doesn't know:
       · who your customer is
       · how you write
       · what's in your inbox
       · what's in your CRM
       · what your offer is

       Without context, AI is a stranger giving you advice.
       
       What we build this weekend = AI WITH context.
       AI that knows you. Your voice. Your customer. Your offer.
```

### [4.2] · BANT scoring · why most leads suck

```
TITLE: Most leads suck. Here's why.

Budget    · can they afford you?
Authority · can they decide?
Need      · do they actually need this?
Timeline  · is now the right moment?

BANT score = how much each prospect matches.
Score < 60 = don't waste a sequence on them.
Score > 80 = stop everything and reach out today.
```

### [4.3] · Apify · the missing piece

```
TITLE: Why does this need Apify?

Claude can read your inbox.
Claude can read your CRM.
Claude CAN'T read LinkedIn (LinkedIn blocks it).

Apify = a scraper that CAN read LinkedIn (legally).
Combine: Apify finds the prospect, Claude reasons about them.

Free tier covers ~50 prospects per week. Plenty for a workshop.
```
